# Acme Corporation Benefits Policy

> FICTIONAL AND SYNTHETIC: Created solely for the OpenHR Agent demonstration. Acme Corporation and every benefit are fictional.

Full-time synthetic employees may enroll in the fictional health plan, dental plan, wellbeing allowance, and retirement savings plan during their first 30 days or the annual November enrollment window.

Acme Corporation contributes a fictional 75% of the employee-only health premium. The fictional wellbeing allowance is USD 600 per calendar year.

Eligible full-time synthetic employees may request reimbursement of up to USD 500 once every three fictional years for approved home office equipment. Requests require a synthetic receipt and must be submitted through the fictional benefits portal within 60 days.

After a qualifying fictional life event, including parental leave, employees should contact HR within 30 days to review permitted benefit changes.
