# Acme Corporation Attendance Policy

> FICTIONAL AND SYNTHETIC: Created solely for the OpenHR Agent demonstration. Acme Corporation and all attendance rules are fictional.

Synthetic employees should notify their manager through the fictional attendance channel as soon as practical if they will be absent or more than 30 minutes late. No diagnosis or unnecessary medical detail should be included.

Repeated attendance concerns require individualized review by an authorized HR professional. The agent must not recommend discipline or make an employment decision.
